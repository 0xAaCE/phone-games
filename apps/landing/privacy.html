Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Phone Games ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and handles information when you interact with our WhatsApp-based game service.

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with Phone Games through WhatsApp, we may collect:

  • Phone number — your WhatsApp phone number, used to identify you as a player and send you game messages.
  • WhatsApp display name — used as your in-game username.
  • Message content — commands and responses you send to the bot (e.g., /start, /vote), used to run the game.
  • Game data — your role assignments, votes, and game outcomes, stored to support gameplay and statistics.

We do not collect any other personal information. We do not access your WhatsApp contacts, profile photo, or message history outside of your interactions with the Phone Games bot.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

  • Operate and deliver the Phone Games service (assigning roles, processing votes, determining winners).
  • Send you game notifications and results via WhatsApp.
  • Improve the game and troubleshoot issues.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

Phone Games relies on the following third-party services to function:

  • Twilio — we use Twilio's WhatsApp Business API to send and receive WhatsApp messages. Your phone number and message content are transmitted through Twilio's infrastructure. See Twilio's Privacy Policy.
  • Meta / WhatsApp — messages are delivered via the WhatsApp platform, operated by Meta. See WhatsApp's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Retention

We retain game data (phone numbers, game history) for as long as needed to operate the service. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us (see Section 7).

5. Data Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data, including encrypted database storage and restricted access controls. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Phone Games is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with their information, please contact us and we will delete it.

7. Your Rights and Contact

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights or for any privacy-related questions, contact us at:

Email: contact@0xaace.xyz

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
